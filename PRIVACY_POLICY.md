**Privacy Policy**

This privacy policy applies to the eBalistyka app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Dmytro Yaroshenko (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as an Open Source service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

**Information Collection and Use**

**The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

All data entered by the user (weapon profiles, ammunition data, sight configurations, environmental conditions) is stored **locally on your device only** using the Application's local database (ObjectBox). No data is sent to the Service Provider or any third party.

The Application does not:
- Collect your IP address
- Track which pages you visit within the Application
- Collect your device location
- Use any analytics or crash reporting services
- Use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies

**Third Party Access**

The Application does not transmit any data to external services. The only network access performed by the Application is:
- Checking for updates (if you manually trigger this action)
- Downloading built-in weapon/ammunition collections (optional, user-initiated)

The Service Provider does not share any user data with third parties because no user data is collected.

**Opt-Out Rights**

Since the Application does not collect any information, no opt-out is necessary. You can stop using the Application at any time by uninstalling it.

**Data Retention Policy**

All data you enter into the Application is stored locally on your device and remains there until you delete the Application or manually remove the data. The Service Provider does not have access to your data.

**Children**

The Application does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The Application does not require any form of registration or personal information to function.

**Security**

The Service Provider does not have access to your data since it remains solely on your device. You are responsible for securing your own device.
