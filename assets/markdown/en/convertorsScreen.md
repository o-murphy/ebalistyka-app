## Converters

A set of tools for quick conversion of ballistic and physical quantities.

Tap any tile to open a converter.

---

### Simple Converters

Enter a value in the top field and select units — all other fields update instantly.

| Converter | Units |
|---|---|
| **Length** | cm, m, inch, foot, yard |
| **Weight** | g, kg, grain, pound, ounce |
| **Pressure** | hPa, mmHg, inHg, psi, bar, atm |
| **Temperature** | °C, °F |
| **Torque** | N·m, ft·lbf, in·lbf |

---

### Advanced Converters

Three converters have extra fields and their own guide (❓ button):

- **Velocity** — convert between m/s, fps, mph, km/h and Mach number with atmosphere support
- **Target Distance** — calculate distance from a target's angular and physical size
- **Angles** — convert between angular units and compute adjustments at a given distance
