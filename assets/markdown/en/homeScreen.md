## Home Screen

![Home Screen](resource:assets/markdown/en/homeScreen_overview.png)

The Home Screen is your main workspace. This is where you enter shooting conditions and get ready-to-use scope adjustments.

---

### Profile & Cartridge

![Profile selector](resource:assets/markdown/homeScreen_profile.png)

- **Profile button** — shows the current profile name. Tap to open the profile list and switch to a different one.
- **Cartridge icon** — quick access to edit the current cartridge.

---

### Environmental Conditions

![Environmental conditions](resource:assets/markdown/en/homeScreen_sideControls.png)

Current atmospheric conditions are displayed on both sides of the wind indicator:

- **Temperature** and **altitude** — left column
- **Humidity** and **atmospheric pressure** — right column

These values are edited on the **Conditions** screen. The ℹ️ button (left) opens shot details, the ❓ button (right) opens this guide.

---

### Wind Direction Indicator

![Wind indicator](resource:assets/markdown/en/homeScreen_wind.png)

A circular dial for setting the **wind direction**:

- **Drag** the arrow — or **swipe** along the circle — to change the direction.
- **Double-tap** the center to reset the direction to zero.
- **Tap the degrees** in the center to enter an exact value manually.
- Direction is shown simultaneously in **degrees** (0–360°) and **clock format** (e.g. 03:00).

---

### Quick Actions

![Quick actions](resource:assets/markdown/en/homeScreen_quickActions.png)

Three buttons below the wind indicator for the most common adjustments:

| Button | Action | Long press |
|---|---|---|
| **Wind Speed** | Opens value input | Resets to 0 |
| **Look Angle** | Opens value input | Resets to 0 |
| **Target Range** | Opens value input | — |

---

### Result Pages

The bottom section is a carousel of three pages. Switch by swiping or tapping the dots at the bottom.

#### Holdovers

![Holdovers](resource:assets/markdown/en/homeScreen_pageHoldovers.png)

Displays scope adjustments for the current conditions:

- **Elevation** — vertical adjustment (up/down)
- **Windage** — horizontal adjustment (left/right)

Values are shown in the units selected in settings (MIL, MOA, click, etc.). Tap the reticle image to open it in full-screen mode.

#### Trajectory Table

![Trajectory table](resource:assets/markdown/en/homeScreen_pageTable.png)

A detailed ballistic data table by distance: velocity, energy, wind drift, time of flight, and more.

#### Trajectory Chart

![Trajectory chart](resource:assets/markdown/en/homeScreen_pageChart.png)

A visual chart of the bullet's trajectory. Lets you quickly assess the cartridge's behaviour at different distances.
