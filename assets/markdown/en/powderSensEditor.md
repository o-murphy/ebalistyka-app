## Powder Sensitivity Measurements

A table of **temperature → muzzle velocity** pairs measured at the range. The app calculates the powder sensitivity coefficient automatically from these measurements and uses it to correct the muzzle velocity at any given temperature.

The calculated sensitivity is shown live above the table in **%/15°C**.

---

### Filling the table

- Enter the ambient (or powder) temperature in the left column and the measured muzzle velocity in the right column
- At least **2 rows** are needed to calculate a coefficient; a single row sets sensitivity to 0
- Rows with zero velocity are ignored on save; the table is sorted by temperature ascending

---

### Tips

- Measure MV at two or more significantly different temperatures (e.g. +5°C and +25°C) for a reliable coefficient
- The first row is pre-filled with the reference MV and temperature from the cartridge settings
