## Weapon Collection

A built-in database of preset rifles and pistols. Tap the **✓ button** (bottom-right of a card) to select it for the current profile.

Each tile shows:
- **Manufacturer** and **model name**
- **Caliber** and **barrel length**
- **Twist rate** and twist direction (right-hand / left-hand)

---

### Search

Type in the search bar to filter by name or manufacturer. The list updates instantly.

---

### Filter

Tap the filter icon (top-right) to narrow the list by **manufacturer**. A badge on the icon indicates an active filter.
