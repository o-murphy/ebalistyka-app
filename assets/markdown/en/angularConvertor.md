## Angle Converter

Converts angular values between different units and calculates linear adjustments at a given distance.

---

### Input Fields

- **Distance** — distance to the target (metres or yards)
- **Angle** — angular value (mil, MOA, mRad, cm/100m, in/100yd, degree)
- **Output unit** — unit for displaying the linear size of the angle at distance (mm, cm, inch, foot)

---

### Results

**Angles** — converts the input value to all units: mil · MOA · mRad · cm/100m · in/100yd · degree.

**Adjustments at distance** — shows how much 1 mil and 1 MOA equal at the entered distance, and the linear size of the entered angle at that distance.

> **Example:** distance 300 m, angle 2 mil → "1 mil at 300 m = 30 cm", "2 mil at 300 m = 60 cm"
