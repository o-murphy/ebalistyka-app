## New Scope / Scope Editor

Fill in the fields to create or update a scope (sight) preset in your library.

---

### General

- **Name** — a label to identify this scope
- **Vendor** — manufacturer name (used for filtering in the scope library)

---

### Mounting

- **Sight height** — distance from the bore axis to the optical axis of the scope; affects elevation calculations
- **Horizontal offset** — lateral displacement of the scope from the bore centre; leave at 0 for centre-mounted optics

---

### Reticle

- **Reticle pattern** — choose an SVG reticle image to display on the targeting screen
- **Focal plane** — FFP (First Focal Plane), SFP (Second Focal Plane), or LWIR (thermal)
- **Min / Max magnification** — zoom range of the scope; used together with the focal plane to correctly scale the reticle at any power setting

---

### Clicks

- **Vertical / Horizontal click** — angular value of one adjustment click
- **Click unit** — unit for click values (MOA, MIL, SMOA, etc.)
