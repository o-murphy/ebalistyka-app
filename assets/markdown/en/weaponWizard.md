## New Weapon / Weapon Editor

Fill in the fields to create or update a weapon preset in your library.

---

### General

- **Name** — a label to identify this weapon
- **Vendor** — manufacturer name (used for filtering in the weapon library)
- **Caliber name** — free-text caliber designation (e.g. "6.5 Creedmoor", ".308 Win")

---

### Ballistics

- **Caliber** — actual bullet diameter; used in ballistic calculations

---

### Hardware

- **Twist rate** — barrel rifling pitch (distance per one full rotation); affects stability calculations for long projectiles
- **Twist direction** — right-hand or left-hand rifling
- **Barrel length** — physical length of the barrel; can be used to estimate muzzle velocity corrections
